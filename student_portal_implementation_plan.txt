1. PROJECT DEFINITION & SCOPE
Purpose:
To build a secure, centralized digital platform that enables students to manage academic, administrative, and communication activities efficiently.

Primary Users:
- Students
- Lecturers
- Administrative Staff
- System Administrators

Core Objectives:
- Centralize student academic data
- Automate administrative processes
- Improve communication
- Ensure data security and privacy
- Provide scalability for future growth


2. FEATURE BREAKDOWN (INDUSTRY STANDARD)

Student Features:
- Authentication & profile management
- Course registration
- Timetable viewing
- Grades & academic records
- Announcements & notifications
- Fee status & payment history

Lecturer Features:
- Course assignment
- Upload grades
- Publish announcements
- View enrolled students

Admin Features:
- Student enrollment management
- Course & department management
- Academic calendar management
- Fee configuration
- Role & permission control

Optional / Advanced:
- Attendance tracking
- Transcript requests
- Document uploads
- Messaging system
- LMS integration
- Analytics dashboard
